WELCOME TO MY TRAVEL DIARY

THATS PLACES I HAVE VISITED

  1. PAAKPATAN

Pakpattan's beauty is multifaceted, encompassing both its historical and natural landscapes, as well as its reputation as a center for spirituality and beauty services. The city is most famously known as the resting place of the Sufi saint, Baba Fariduddin Ganjshakar, and its shrine draws millions of devotees. Beyond its spiritual significance, Pakpattan's fertile plains and proximity to the Sutlej River contribute to its scenic beauty and agricultural richness. Additionally, Pakpattan has a growing beauty industry, with salons like Beauty Hive and Infinity Beauty Salon offering services such as HydraFacials, keratin treatments, and more.

  • MALAMAJABBA

    • Malam Jabba is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, offering stunning views of snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and dense forests. It's a popular destination for both winter sports enthusiasts and those seeking a serene escape amidst nature.

  • NARAN KAGHAN

    • Naran and Kaghan valleys are renowned for their stunning natural beauty, making them popular tourist destinations in Pakistan. The area is characterized by lush green landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and pristine lakes, offering breathtaking scenery. Naran, situated in the upper Kaghan Valley, is a picturesque town known for its subarctic climate and vibrant summer atmosphere.

  • ISLAMABAD

    • Islamabad is renowned for its natural beauty, harmonious blend of modern development and nature, and well-planned infrastructure. It's often cited as one of the most beautiful capital cities in the world, with its lush greenery, wide avenues, and the backdrop of the Margalla Hills.

  • MALL ROAD MUSOORIE

    • Mall Road in Mussoorie is the city's main shopping and tourist hub, known for its colonial architecture, viewpoints, and lively atmosphere. It stretches from Picture Palace to the Library, offering a 2 km stretch of shops, restaurants, and colonial-era buildings. Tourists often visit for shopping, enjoying the views of the Doon Valley, and experiencing the bustling ambiance.
